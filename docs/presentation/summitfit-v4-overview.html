The v4.0 Update

A faster, more accessible, more cohesive site — now with online tour booking built straight into Ernest's Google Calendar.

Accessibility · Speed · Design · Online Booking

What's New

1

Accessible to everyone

Screen-reader labels, full keyboard navigation, readable contrast and larger tap targets across the whole site — now meeting recognised accessibility standards, with zero critical issues.

2

Noticeably faster

The core code bundle shrank ~60% (648 KB → 258 KB), images were right-sized (~450 KB saved) and the Facebook feed no longer slows loads. Quicker first paint, especially on mobile.

3

One cohesive design

A single unified header on every page (full menu now reachable everywhere), snappier animations, and consistent midnight-and-cyan branding throughout.

4

Online booking

Clients book a tour on the site, choosing a real slot from Ernest's Google Calendar. Bookings flow automatically into the admin console for approval.

How a Booking Works

1

Sign in with Google — one click, free account.

2

Pick a tour, then a date & time from Ernest's live calendar.

3

Google books it instantly and emails client & Ernest.

4

Appears in Admin within ~10 min, marked Pending.

5

Ernest confirms payment, sets it Confirmed.

Why Pending first? There's no online card payment yet, so every booking is held for Ernest's manual approval once payment is arranged directly with the client. When a payment gate is added later, this can switch to auto-confirm.

Admin Console — the essentials

A

Getting in

Go to /admin and sign in with the info@summitfitadventures.com Google account. Only the owner account sees the admin; clients get their own booking dashboard.

B

The one workflow that matters

In Bookings, new website bookings arrive tagged “Via Cal Page” as Pending. Once you've taken payment, switch the status to Confirmed. Cancelling in Google Calendar auto-cancels here too.

Full step-by-step: see docs/ADMIN-GUIDE.md — covers Clients, Guides, Pricing, Routes, Specials and What's New, plus a troubleshooting FAQ.
